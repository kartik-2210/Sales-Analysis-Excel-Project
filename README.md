# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Dashboard | Excel Dashboard Project

## Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive sales analysis of Vrinda Store using Microsoft Excel. The objective of this analysis was to understand customer purchasing behavior, identify sales trends, analyze business performance, and generate actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making and improve overall sales growth.

The project involved data cleaning, data processing, advanced pivot table analysis, dashboard development, and visualization of key performance metrics through an interactive Excel dashboard.

---

## Raw Dataset
The project utilizes a retail sales dataset containing over 31,000+ transactional records including customer demographics, order details, sales channels, product categories, order status, and state-wise sales information. The dataset was used to perform data cleaning, processing, analysis, and dashboard creation to uncover meaningful business insights and customer purchasing patterns.

### Dataset Includes
- Customer Gender & Age Group
- Order IDs & Order Status
- Sales Amount & Revenue Data
- Product Categories
- Sales Channels (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.)
- State-wise Sales Information
- Monthly Sales & Orders Data

## Key Business Questions Answered
- Compare sales and orders using a single chart
- Which month recorded the highest sales and orders?
- Who purchased more in 2022 — men or women?
- What were the different order statuses in 2022?
- Which top 10 states contributed the most to sales?
- What is the relationship between age and gender based on number of orders?
- Which sales channel contributed the highest sales?
- Which product category had the highest sales?

---

## Dashboard Features
The interactive dashboard provides:
- Sales vs Orders Analysis
- Men vs Women Sales Comparison
- Order Status Distribution
- Top 10 Performing States
- Age vs Gender Analysis
- Channel-wise Sales Contribution
- Dynamic Filtering using Slicers
- Interactive Data Exploration

---

## Key Insights Derived
- Women customers contributed approximately 65% of total purchases, making them the dominant customer segment.
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh emerged as the top 3 contributing states, accounting for nearly 35% of total sales.
- Adult customers aged 30–49 years contributed around 50% of total orders, making them the most valuable customer group.
- Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra were the leading sales channels, contributing nearly 80% of total sales.

---

## Final Business Recommendation
To improve sales performance and customer engagement, Vrinda Store should focus on targeting women customers aged 30–49 years residing in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh by offering personalized promotions, discounts, and coupons through major e-commerce platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
